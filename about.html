About Robotics Club

Welcome fellow tech learner! This is an introduction to the Robotics Club! We aim to assist beginners to--well, robotics! However, experienced builders are also very much welcome!

Our club fosters collaboration, critical thinking, and creativity through exciting challenges and competitions.

Take note that this club will be highly competitive, with differing sections depending if you are a beginner or expert. Experts will be put on "Advanced"; Beginners will be assigned to "Newbies". You will rank up based on your skill when we host competitions! :D
